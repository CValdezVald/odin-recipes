Simple Apple Pie


Description

A simple apple pie that I enjoy eating at home will be using store bought dough

    Ingredients

  • 7 large apples of your choosing
  • 1/2 cup of Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • store bought dough
  • 1 egg white

Steps

  1. Begin by setting your oven to 400F and having rack in the middle
  2. In a large bowl combine your sliced and peeled apples with all the dry Ingredients
  3. Remove store bought pie crust from packaging and lay it out in a deep dish pie plate
  4. pour apple slices into the pie dish and cover with your second pie crust
  5. make sure to put holes in pie crust to let hot air escape while baking
  6. Brush surface with beaten egg white and foil edges to allow for even cook
  7. bake pie for 25min remove foil then bake until whole pie is even golden brown(30-40min)
