Tofu Fried Rice

Description

easy 1 pan tofu fried rice I make on a daily basis

Ingredients

  • 1/2 block of tofu
  • 2 table spoons of soy sauce
  • 2 cups of frozen greens
  • 1 cup of day old white rice
  • 1 table spoon of oil
  • ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. in a pan add oil let it warm up then add frozen veggies
  2. dice and dry your tofu with a paper towel and add to the pan
  3. introduce rice and soy sauce into the pan
  4. after letting the ingredients mix for a few minutes add pepper
