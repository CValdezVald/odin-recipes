Asada Tacos

Description

Easy to follow recipe for asada tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of thin sliced skirt steak
  • 4 limes
  • salt to taste
  • 1 whole white onion
  • garlic powder to taste
  • a table spoon of oil
  • store bought corn tortillas
  • 1 bunch of cilantr
  • 1 cabbage

Steps

  1. in a bowl add steak and add seasonsing and 4 limes let marinate for 15min or longer
  2. in a large pot add oil and let it reach cooking temp
  3. add peeled onion halved
  4. add steaks into pot and cover till steaks are almost fully cooked
  5. remove steak from pot and dice into small pieces then add it back to the pot
  6. cook for another 15-30min till meat is softened
  7. in a nonstick pan add a drizzle of oil and cook corn tortillas till slighly browned
  8. cut cabbage into small strips and dice the cilantro
  9. build taco and add toppings goes good with a green salsa
